About our collaboration

At 1099Workers.com and UtilityConnect, we are excited to announce a transformative collaboration that's set to redefine the landscape of support for independent contractors and individuals on the move. Our shared mission and complementary strengths create a powerful synergy, enhancing the lives of those we serve.

Our collaboration marries the cutting-edge technology of Utility Connect™ with the deep understanding of the gig economy provided by 1099Workers.com. Together, we aim to:

Enhance Financial Security

By offering tailored benefits and streamlined moving solutions, we empower self-employed individuals to achieve greater financial security.

Simplify Life Transitions

Moving can be stressful, but our partnership strives to make it smoother and more efficient, ensuring peace of mind during life transitions.

Promote Well-Being

We are committed to supporting the well-being of our users, both in their work and personal lives, through our combined efforts.

Create Value

Our collaboration generates value by aligning the expertise of Utility Connect™ with the specific needs of the self-employed, creating a win-win for all involved.
