At 1099Workers.com, our mission is to empower the self-employed with access to essential and tailored employee benefits that promote financial security, well-being, and peace of mind. We are dedicated to revolutionizing the gig economy by providing comprehensive and flexible benefit solutions, supporting the unique needs and aspirations of independent contractors, freelancers, and temporary workers.

Core Values

By adhering to these core values, 1099Workers.com strives to be a trusted partner and advocate for gig workers, helping them thrive in their independent careers while enjoying the benefits traditionally associated with traditional employment.

Empowerment

We believe in empowering gig workers to take control of their financial well-being and career by offering them access to tailored employee benefits that support their unique lifestyles and aspirations.

Inclusivity

We embrace diversity and inclusivity, ensuring that our benefit solutions are accessible and relevant to all gig workers, regardless of their industry, background, or geographic location.

Innovation

We continuously strive to innovate and improve our benefit offerings, leveraging cutting-edge technology and insights to deliver the most effective and relevant solutions for gig workers in the ever-evolving gig economy.

Integrity

Trust and transparency are at the heart of our interactions with gig workers and partners. We uphold the highest ethical standards and prioritize honesty in all our communications and services.

Advocacy

We are passionate advocates for gig workers, championing their rights and well-being within the gig economy and beyond. We work to ensure that gig workers receive the recognition and support they deserve.

Collaboration

We foster a collaborative environment, working closely with gig workers, employers, and industry experts to co-create benefit solutions that address the unique challenges and opportunities faced by gig workers.

Customer-Centricity

Gig workers are at the center of everything we do. We listen to their needs, seek their feedback, and continuously improve our offerings to provide exceptional experiences and outcomes.

Social Responsibility

We are committed to making a positive impact in the gig economy and the communities we serve. Through responsible business practices and philanthropic initiatives, we aim to contribute to a more equitable and sustainable future for gig workers.

Breaking barriers and Changing lives

1099Workers.com is the premier provider of employee benefits for gig workers, offering a wide range of customizable benefit options designed to meet the diverse needs of modern independent contractors. With our innovative platform and expert guidance, gig workers can gain financial stability, protect their future, and confidently pursue their passions, knowing that 1099Workers.com has their back every step of the way.
